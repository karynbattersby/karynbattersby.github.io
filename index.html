My Why is to Inspire you to live your “Big Life” this is why my company exists.

My Values.

Which permeate everything I do, are truth, freedom, authenticity, connection, courage and growth.

I’d like to expand on what they mean to me: 

Connection: We choose to connect. When you connect, everything is possible

Courage: We choose the courageous option, that’s where growth lives. Courageous conversations, courageous actions.

Growth: We choose growth because as humans we are happiest when we are growing.

Truth: We choose to put truth first. In all that we say and all that we do, we will speak the truth and we will enact the truth.

Freedom: We choose freedom of choice, freedom of expression, freedom to be authentically yourself

Authenticity: We choose authenticity, our words and actions are aligned. We allow ourselves to be seen

Our Mission:

We believe that everybody has a unique purpose or gift to bring to the world, We are here to help you bring yours to the world. 

Pay it forward:

Like a suspended coffee only it’s personal growth and development!
That’s right, if you are in the fortunate position to have a little more than you need financially, you can sponsor someone else’s growth by paying it forward.
